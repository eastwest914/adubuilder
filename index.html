Frequently Asked Questions

What is a prebuilt ADU?

A prebuilt Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) is a factory-built secondary home that is transported to your property for installation. It offers a faster and more efficient alternative to traditional on-site construction while providing a fully functional living space.

How long does it take to install a prebuilt ADU?

The timeline depends on factors such as site preparation, permitting, and utility connections. Once the unit is manufactured and delivered, installation is typically much faster than traditional home construction.

Do I need a permit for a prebuilt ADU?

Yes. Most jurisdictions require permits for ADU installation. Permit requirements vary by city and county, and our team can help guide you through the permitting process.

How much does a prebuilt ADU cost?

The cost depends on the size, floor plan, customization options, site conditions, and installation requirements. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your project.

Can I customize my ADU?

Yes. We offer a variety of customization options including floor plans interior finishes, cabinetry, flooring, fixtures, and exterior designs, allowing you to create a space that suits your style and needs.
